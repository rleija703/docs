## General

**What is Gextron?**  
Gextron is a real-time options analytics dashboard that surfaces key levels, price targets, option flow, and live tape to help traders anticipate market moves.

**Who is Gextron for?**  
Retail traders, active day traders, swing traders, and analysts who want institutional-grade tools.

## Lookups

**What counts as a lookup?**  
Opening a ticker counts as 1 lookup. Refreshing within 60 seconds does not count as an additional lookup.

**How do I track my lookup usage?**
